Our Fantastic Sponsors

Lockheed Martin

Lockheed Martin not only supports us financially, but also provides us a team mentor, Tom Convery. He shares the company's respect for leadership, community, and technology, and encourages us to do the same.

Bechtel

Bechtel’s high regards inspire our team to keep their qualities in mind when planning, building, and competing alike with their generous financial support and assistance.

Oakton Boosters & Community

The Oakton PTSA are appreciated for their financial support, helping the club expand.


Leidos

Leidos and their innovation of technology inspire us to work hard. The support they provide for us is something we cherish as much as any other sponsor.

IEEE

IEEE's core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. IEEE helps Oakton Robotics strive to improve.
