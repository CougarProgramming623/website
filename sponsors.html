Oakton High School Robotics Sponsors

We rely on our sponsors to help Cougar Robotics reach for new heights. They provide us with financial and technical support, and ensure that we have everything we need in order to be a strong and successful team. Thanks to their generous contributions, through funds, tools, materials, mentors, and more, we are able to maintain our competitive edge and strive for greatness.

Cougar Robotics is a 501c3 organization, meaning that all contributions to our club are tax deductable. If you are interested in becoming a sponsor, please email us at the address below!

Alarm.com

Alarm.com provides smart home and business security solutions. They offer technology for things like security systems, video surveillance, and home automation, all controlled through a mobile app.

Optimist Club of Vienna

Vienna Optomists are a Non-Profit Organzation supporting the youth by providing support and donations.

Reckon

Reckon is a software company that provides desktop and cloud-based accounting software for accountants, bookkeepers, small to medium businesses, and personal users. They offer solutions like accounting software, payroll, business loans, and clinic management software.

Oakton High School PTSA

Our school's PTSA helps us financially and by promoting the club to students and parents. They also help us secure meeting times at school during the weekend.

Salesforce.com

Salesforce.com is a cloud-based software company that helps businesses manage customer relationships. They provide tools for sales, customer service, marketing, and analytics, all designed to improve how companies interact with their customers.

Capital One

Capital One is a financial services company that offers credit cards, loans, banking, and savings accounts. They focus on using technology and data to make banking and financial services more accessible and personalized for customers.

Metric 5/Malik Consulting Inc

NS2

NS2 is business that provides secure cloud and software solutions tailored for U.S. national security and critical infrastructure clients. They offer enterprise applications, analytics, and database services with enhanced security to meet the unique requirements of these sectors.

Cooley LLP

Cooley LLP is a global law firm that specializes in working with innovative companies, startups, and investors. They provide legal services in areas like technology, life sciences, intellectual property, and corporate law.

Vienna Wireless Society (VWS)

The Vienna Wireless Society (VWS) is a radio organization based in Vienna, Virginia. With over 300 members, VWS promotes the development of skills and knowledge in radio communications and electronic technology through various activities, including operating events, educational programs, and public service emergency communications.
