The business team is the newest addition to our robotics team founded by Aspen Harter in 2018. They are responsible for community outreach, sponsorships, engineering notebook, scouting, and social media. The business team members are on the team for both FTC and FRC. It is a much smaller team of only 6-10 members. They attend every competition and talk to other teams for possible collaborations for outreach events and scout for alliances.
